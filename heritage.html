Mumbai Heritage Sites

Mumbai is home to numerous heritage sites that attract visitors from all over the world. These iconic landmarks reflect the city's rich history and cultural diversity. Whether you're admiring the architectural splendor of the **Gateway of India** or exploring the fascinating history of the **Elephanta Caves**, Mumbai offers an unforgettable experience for history enthusiasts. Don't forget to visit **Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its magnificent Victorian Gothic architecture.
