Welcome to Mumbai Tourism

Mumbai, often referred to as the "City of Dreams," is the financial and entertainment capital of India. Located on the west coast of the country, Mumbai is the capital of the Indian state of Maharashtra and is one of the most populous cities in the world. Known for its bustling streets, iconic landmarks, and vibrant culture, Mumbai attracts millions of visitors every year, both from within India and abroad.
